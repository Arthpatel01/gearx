Quality Controls

Quality Controls

PFMEA

Potential failure conditions associated with the repair process are identified and counter measures are implemented to mitigate risks.

This includes a detailed analysis of the repair process, identifying potential failure points, and developing corrective measures to prevent these failures from occurring.

Root -Cause Analysis

Received product is evaluated for root cause and required repairs are quoted to the customer for approval.

This involves identifying the root cause of a failure, so that corrective measures can be taken to prevent it from happening again.

5 Whys Warranty Analysis

If a repair fails during our warranty period, the returned product is evaluated and corrective action established.

This involves using the 5 Whys technique to identify the root cause of the failure, so that corrective measures can be taken to prevent it from happening again.

Product Testing to Failure

We frequently test products to failure so our customers can be assured of product life expectations.
